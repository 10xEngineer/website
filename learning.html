We are re-engineering education!


How do you grow a real engineer inside you? Formal education is outdated and can hardly keep up with the advances in technology, programming and computer science in general. Looking for a change? Welcome to the world of 10xEngineer.me - online training platform that will boost your skills in easy to use and effective way.

Platform is currently in private alpha testing. We expect to launch by the 1Q/2013.

Web-based development environment

Apply what you have learnt by writing real code. Anytime, anywhere. Our online development environment is the fastest way how to start practicing newly acquired knowledge. No more hurdles with local dependencies.

We are engineers and made editor to match our daily demanding workflow:

  • Real-time syntax highlighting for popular programming languages.
  • Uses keyboard shortcuts for fast interaction.
  • Customizable themes.

Integrated Compiler

Compile and run the programs written in programming languages like Java, Python and Javascript. Right from your browser. Anything from one-liner to a complex project can be offloaded to our compute cluster available on demand.

Customize compilation engine to provide support for your framework or online application.

Online training Labs and assesment

Asses your knowledge using our Labs technology, providing isolated and safe sandbox for your experiments. Our platform will evaluate the changes without need to upload code anywhere.

Save the change and review feedback, including suggestion of areas to focus on and additional resources.
